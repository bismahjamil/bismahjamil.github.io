About Me

My research explores the fascinating world of symmetry analysis, a dynamic approach that unlocks exact solutions to differential equations and finds real-world applications in geodesic systems, fluid flow, and heat transfer.

Academic Background

Ph.D. in Mathematics from the School of Natural Sciences, National University of Sciences and Technology, Islamabad.

Research Identity

Symmetry analysis, Lie point symmetries, Noether symmetries, optimal systems, conservation laws and group-invariant solutions.

Academic Recognition

President’s Gold Medal for M.Phil. Mathematics and NUST Special Scholarship for outstanding graduate performance.
