Academic Profile

Dr. Bismah Jamil is a mathematician whose research focuses on symmetry analysis, Noether symmetries, Lie point symmetries, optimal systems and conservation laws for differential equations.

Research Vision

Exploring how symmetry methods reveal exact solutions and conservation structures hidden inside differential equations.

Applications

Geodesic systems, fluid flow, heat transfer, mathematical physics, liquid film flow and magnetohydrodynamic models.

Academic Identity

Mathematician, researcher and educator with university teaching experience and a strong publication record.

Research Interests

Core areas connecting analytical, geometrical and physical aspects of differential equations.
