About Me

My research explores symmetry analysis as a powerful method for finding exact solutions of differential equations and understanding conservation laws in geometry and mathematical physics.

Academic Background

Ph.D. Mathematics, National University of Sciences and Technology, Islamabad. Dissertation: Noether Symmetries, Corresponding Conservation Laws and Applications.

Research Focus

Lie point symmetries, Noether symmetries, optimal systems, group-invariant solutions, geodesic equations, conservation laws, heat transfer and fluid flow.
